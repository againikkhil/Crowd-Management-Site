In this website, our main purpose is to make medical services better so that every section of society can access these facilities and treatments more easily and efficiently.
We have made this website so easy that anyone can book their appointment slot from the comfort of their own place. Users can book an appointment using their Aadhaar or Ration card by simply taking a picture of their available document. If they are unable to log in using their mobile number, there are alternative options available.
This could include saving time and money, reducing stress, improving access to information or services, and making it easier to manage large crowds or busy events.
